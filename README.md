## Vision

Terranova combines epoch-based civilization progression (inspired by Empire Earth) with voxel terrain and biomes (inspired by Minecraft) and deep economic simulation (inspired by Anno/Settlers). Every playthrough unfolds on a unique, procedurally generated world where your decisions shape the landscape across millennia.
